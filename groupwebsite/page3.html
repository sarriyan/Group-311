Our Halal Commitment

Yo-Delish products are certified Halal by the Halal Hub Division of the Department of Islamic Development Malaysia (JAKIM), Selangor Islamic Religious Department (JAIS) and other relevant recognized Foreign Halal Certification Bodies and Authorities.

Our manufacturing processes reflect an understanding that Halal food and drinks are important to every Muslim consumer.

Yo-Delish is deeply committed to upholding these standards of Halal and in preserving customer’s confidence in every product that is produced at the DLMI plant. As a Halal dairy products manufacturer, our production facilities are subject to regular routine, comprehensive site visits by JAKIM and JAIS enforcement officers.

Every local Yo-Delish product bears the official JAKIM Halal logo on its pack. Imported Yo-Delish products are certified Halal by the country of origin’s Halal Certification Bodies that is recognized by JAKIM.

Beyond certifying our product’s Halal status, this logo assures consumers that Yo-Delish adhere to strict hygienic practices, starting from hygiene of personnel and processing to transportation and storage.

This affirms the brand’s longstanding good reputation in a discerning and competitive market.
