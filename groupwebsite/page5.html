Will L.casei-01 die after Yo-Delish is opened ?

Our infant L. casei-01 is heat-sensitive. Yoyic should be consumed as soon as possible after opening because it is only active between 2 and 10°C. You can only maintain L. casei-01's energy in this manner! P.S. Serve cold for optimal results!

There is no color additive in Yo-Delish. Why is it pale yellow in color ?

Can Yo-Delish be heated ?

It cannot be heated, sorry. Once heated, L. casei-01 will perish. Heating has an impact on Yoyic's flavor in addition to L. casei-01's effects. Yoyic from the refrigerator can be removed and allowed to come to room temperature for 30 minutes before consumption if you feel it is too cold.
